Instructions on how to use the calendar
  • First, you CHOOSE any of the days on the calendar
    		•
  • Click on the particular day you want to set your to-do-lists on
    		•
  • You will enter into a page with all the time slots from 12am - 11pm of the day you selected
    		•
  • Set your plans on that particular time slot and click on the to save what you have input
    		•
  • Click on the to go back to this page (But BEWARE! Once you click cancel every data that you have input will be gone
    		•
  • Hover over the dates in the Legend box will provide the details on what does the colors represents
    		•
